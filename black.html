Our Services

Security Guards

Our SIA-licensed security guards provide a strong, professional presence to deter threats, protect assets, and ensure a safe environment for your business, property, or event.

Mobile Security Patrols

For businesses and properties needing a flexible yet effective security solution, our mobile patrols offer rapid response and visible deterrence, keeping your premises secure 24/7.

Event Security

From corporate functions to festivals, our experienced event security teams manage access control, crowd safety, and risk mitigation, ensuring your event runs smoothly and securely.

School Security

We offer specialized security solutions for schools, ensuring the safety of students, staff, and visitors with vigilant security personnel trained for educational environments.

Key Holding & Alarm Response

Let us take the stress out of security with our key holding and alarm response services. We provide rapid, professional responses to alarm activations, ensuring your property remains protected at all times.

Lock and Unlock Service

Trust our security professionals to securely open and close your premises at designated times, ensuring only authorized access and reducing the risk of security breaches.

Bars & Nightclub Security

Our experienced door supervisors maintain a safe and controlled environment at bars and nightclubs, ensuring smooth entry management and handling incidents professionally.

Close Protection

For high-profile individuals, executives, and VIPs, we provide discreet, highly trained close protection operatives to ensure personal safety and risk mitigation in all situations.
